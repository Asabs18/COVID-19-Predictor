Overview

This site showcases a small and simple prediction algorithm used to try and predict future COVID-19 cases in our community. Who are we? We are a group of students going to Haldane Highschool in Coldspring, NY. We created this project in our computer science class from around March - April of 2021. The intent of this project was mainly educational so none of the graphs or predictions on this site should be taken as fact or reliable source.

Whats Under the Hood?

This project collects data from https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/covid19/index.htm, parses that data and passes the relevant infromation through a Recurrent Neural Network, More specifically a Long-Short-Term-Memory model to try and predict future COVID-19 cases in our county. That prediction along with the original data is then passed to this website where it is displayed in a quick easy to understand format. This entire project automatically updates to show the most recent data whenever possible.
